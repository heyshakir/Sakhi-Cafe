❓ Frequently Asked Questions
Do you take reservations?
Yes! You can reserve a table by calling us, WhatsApping, or filling the contact form above. Walk-ins are also welcome.
Do you offer home delivery?
We partner with major food delivery platforms. You can also call us directly for delivery inquiries within our service zone.
Is there a kids menu?
Absolutely! Our kid-friendly menu includes smaller portions, non-spicy items, and yummy treats just for little ones.
Do you host private events?
Yes! We offer private café reservations for birthdays, corporate meetings, and special gatherings. Contact us for details.
Do you have vegetarian/vegan options?
We have a wide variety of vegetarian options and can accommodate dietary requirements. Please inform your server.
